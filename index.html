about

I grew up in Scotland and somehow ended up on a beach in Burleigh Heads — which still feels like getting away with something. I started out in forensic science, fell hard for code somewhere along the way, and never looked back.

These days I'm Head of Technology at MXstore, Australia's biggest motocross store. I look after the engineering team and the systems underneath it — most recently rebuilding our data platform on Oracle HeatWave and OCI. I still write open source when something annoys me enough to fix it.

I'm not someone who's always making something. But every so often the urge strikes, and when it does I want a home for whatever falls out. This is that home — small, occasional, and mine.
